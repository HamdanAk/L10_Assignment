Cases in Singapore

Total Cases: 257,510

In Hospital: 1,365

Total Deaths: 678


The number of Covid 19 cases in Singapore has grown to 257,510, with 2,079 new cases reported on Dec 14.


Of these, 2,030 were reported in the community and 40 in the workers' dormitories. The remaining 9 cases were imported.


A further 2,275 patients were discharged, including 366 seniors aged above 60. A total of 238,909 cases are recovered to date.

9 out of 10 Singaporeans have been vaccinated
